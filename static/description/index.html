Customizable products are the product in which you offer a base product to the customers and they can further personalize it as per their requirements. For instance, your customer wishes to purchase a tshirt with a print of their name. It is not possible for you to print tshirts with all the names available worldwide. Hence, when you offer customizable tshirts then your customer can add their name to get the print of their name in the tshirt

Now you can offer customizable products on your Odoo Website. Odoo Web to Print allows your customers to customize products using text and image. Your customers can upload the image from local and or add text in the image from Odoo Website. Hence, the customers can purchase the customized products from Odoo Website.

Manage the Customizable Products in Your Odoo Website

The module offers you a way to provide the customizable products in your Odoo Website. You can simply distinguish the products which you want to offer as customizable products from Odoo backend. Moreover, these customizable products can be identified by an icon in Odoo Website Shop Page.

Your customer can easily add the text in the product image and upload the image as well. The customers can customize the product from front side as well as backside.

Moreover, the customers can drag the text as well as image in any section of the product as per their choice. Once done with the customization, the customers can add the customized product to the cart and then make the further purchase

Detailed Features List

Here is the detailed list of features for Odoo Web to Print

  • Make customizable products in Odoo Website
  • Customer can add text and upload images to the product
  • Set the font size, color and style of the text to be added
  • You need to set the customizable area of the product
  • Customizable products are distinguished in the Shop Page with the help of an icon
  • You can preview the customized product image from orders
  • Configure the terms and conditions from backend
  • Website View
  • Backend View
